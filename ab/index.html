Private Events

Private events are used for individuals who can book venues such as.
"Wedding" "Wedding receptions", "Birthday parties", "Festival gatherings"

Private events are used for individuals who want certain guests to arrive at the venue. Private events will have a guest list to ensure the venue is not open to the public.

Corporate Events

Corporate events are used for a business to promote their companies brand and products such as. "Business dinners", "Conferences", "Networking events", "Seminars", "Product launches" "Meetings".

Corporate events can be used by businesses of all sizes to solicit new business, create a corporate or brand image, and retain and build loyalty with existing suppliers and customers.
